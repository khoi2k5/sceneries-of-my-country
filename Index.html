Beautiful Cities And Places Around My country

I live in Vietnam, where has alots of beautiful places and cities, here are some of favorite places:

  • Ha long Bay
  • Mount Ba Den
  • Da Lat -City of Eternal Spring

  • Ha Long Bay

    src
    • Recognized by UNESCO as a world cultural heritage site
    • Ha Long Bay is famous for its spectacular caves, many of which can be explored by visitors
    • Ha Long Bay features over 1,600 limestone islands and islets, most of which are uninhabited and untouched by humans

  • Mount Ba Den

    src

    The Buddha on Mount Ba Den is called the "Bodhisattva Buddha Statue" (Bức tượng Phật Bà Tây Bổ Đà Sơn).

    • Mount Ba Den is home to Ba Den Pagoda, a famous Buddhist pilgrimage site
    • Mount Ba Den is a popular spot for hiking and adventure tourism
    • The area around the mountain is rich in biodiversity, with tropical forests that house various plant and animal species.

  • Da Lat -City of Eternal Spring

    src

    It is often called the "City of Eternal Spring" due to its pleasant weather year-round, with mild days and cool nights.

    • Its French-style villas, European gardens, and colonial architecture give the city a unique charm
    • Da Lat sits at an elevation of 1,500 meters (4,900 feet) above sea level, giving it a cool climate unlike most of Vietnam.
    • Da Lat is known as Vietnam’s flower capital. It is home to a variety of flowers like hydrangeas, sunflowers, roses, and the iconic cherry blossoms.
